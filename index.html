2Summary

The app does not transmit personal data to Salman Reda Cars Sud, a developer server, or an advertising or analytics service.

It has no internet permission, account system, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking technology, crash-reporting service, or background data service. Its main features work offline.

3Information processed on your device

If you use the rental-estimate feature, you may enter your name, phone number, pickup date, pickup time, pickup location, rental duration, selected vehicle, and optional extras.

The app uses these details on your device to calculate an estimate and create a PDF when you request one. The app does not submit a reservation, send a message to Salman Reda Cars Sud, take payment, or check live vehicle availability.

4Local storage and retention

Saved vehicle identifiers are kept in the app's private preferences on your device. They remain until you remove them, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

When you create an estimate, the app writes a PDF containing the details you entered. On Android 10 or later, it is placed in the device's shared Downloads collection. On older supported Android versions, it is placed in the app's external files area.

The PDF filename may include a cleaned version of the customer name and vehicle name. The PDF is a planning estimate only and is not a confirmed booking, rental agreement, receipt, or payment record. The app does not retain a separate server copy.

You control retention. Remove saved vehicles in the app, delete PDFs with your file manager, clear the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app.

5PDF viewers and user-directed disclosure

After creating a PDF, the app asks Android to open it. If you select a PDF-viewer or file-viewer app, Android gives that app temporary read access to the file.

A PDF saved in shared Downloads may also be accessible to you and to apps that have appropriate access to that folder. Those external apps operate under their own privacy policies.

Salman Reda Cars Sud does not choose, control, or receive data from the viewer you select.

6Permissions, third-party code, and backups

The app declares no internet permission and requests no Android runtime permissions. It uses Android system storage APIs only when you choose to generate a PDF.

Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer are disabled for the app's data.

The app includes AndroidX, Google Material Components, and Glide software libraries for interface and local image display. In this app they are not configured with an analytics, advertising, tracking, account, or network service.

7Security

The app limits processing to the device and does not transmit the information you enter. You are responsible for protecting access to your device and locally saved PDFs.

Avoid entering information you do not want stored in a PDF, and delete files you no longer need.

8Children

This vehicle-rental planning app is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

The app does not knowingly transmit or receive personal information from children.

9Your choices

You may use the catalogue, search, filters, and calculator without creating an account. You may choose not to enter personal details or generate a PDF.

Because the app does not transmit your information to Salman Reda Cars Sud, there is no remote app account or server record for us to retrieve or delete.
