1. Who we are

This policy applies to the Salman Reda Cars Sud Android app, package name com.salmanredacars.sud. The app is provided by SALMAN REDA CARS SUD, HAY EL WAHDA RDC N1738, Dakhla (M), Morocco. Commercial Register: 31701. ICE: 003915533000036.

2. Summary

The app optionally collects information that you enter, including your name and phone number, solely to provide the rental-estimate and PDF features that you request. This information is processed and stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to Salman Reda Cars Sud, a developer server, or an advertising or analytics service.

The app has no internet permission, account system, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking technology, crash-reporting service, or background data service. Its main features work offline.

3. Information processed on your device

If you choose to use the rental-estimate feature, the app collects the name, phone number, pickup date, pickup time, pickup location, rental duration, selected vehicle, and optional extras that you enter. The collection is optional because you can use the catalogue and other app features without providing these details. The information is used only for app functionality: calculating an estimate and creating a PDF at your request.

The app does not submit a reservation, send these details to Salman Reda Cars Sud, take payment, or check live vehicle availability.

4. Local storage and retention

Saved vehicle identifiers are kept in the app's private preferences on your device. They remain until you remove them, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

When you create an estimate, the app writes a PDF containing the details you entered. On Android 10 or later, it is placed in the device's shared Downloads collection. On older supported Android versions, it is placed in the app's external files area. The PDF filename may include a cleaned version of the customer name and vehicle name. The PDF is a planning estimate only and is not a confirmed booking, rental agreement, receipt, or payment record. The app does not retain a separate server copy.

You control retention. Remove saved vehicles in the app, delete PDFs with your file manager, clear the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app.

5. PDF viewers and user-directed disclosure

After creating a PDF, the app asks Android to open it. This user-directed action transfers the PDF, including the information you entered, to the PDF-viewer or file-viewer app that you choose. Android gives that app temporary read access to the file. A PDF saved in shared Downloads may also be accessible to you and to apps that have appropriate access to that folder.

External viewer and file-management apps operate under their own privacy policies. Salman Reda Cars Sud does not choose, control, or receive data from the viewer you select.

6. Permissions, third-party code, and backups

The app declares no internet permission and requests no Android runtime permissions. It uses Android system storage APIs only when you choose to generate a PDF. Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer are disabled for the app's data.

The app includes AndroidX, Google Material Components, and Glide software libraries for interface and local image display. In this app they are not configured with an analytics, advertising, tracking, account, or network service.

7. Security

The app does not transmit your information over the internet or to our servers. Information is transferred to another app only when you direct Android to open the generated PDF in a viewer. Android grants the selected app controlled read access to the file.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device and locally saved PDFs. Avoid entering information you do not want stored in a PDF, and delete files you no longer need.

8. Children

This vehicle-rental planning app is intended for adults and is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly transmit or receive personal information from children.

9. Your choices

You may use the catalogue, search, filters, and calculator without creating an account. You may choose not to enter your name, phone number, or other rental details and not to generate a PDF.

You can delete a generated PDF at any time using your device's file manager. You can remove saved vehicles in the app, clear the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app. Because the app does not send your information to Salman Reda Cars Sud, there is no remote app account or server record for us to retrieve or delete.
